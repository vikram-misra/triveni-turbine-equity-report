02

The name, across every lens I'd be asked about

Business & Strategy

A quality, asset-light franchise

Domestic leader in sub-100 MW industrial steam turbines, ~56% promoter-held. The moat is the installed base: an annuity-like aftermarket and refurbishment stream that compounds with every unit shipped.

  • Enquiry pipeline reported roughly doubling YoY
  • Export push across Europe, US, SE Asia
  • Optionality: data-center power, SMRs, geothermal, CO₂-based turbines
Financials

Compounding, with one thing to watch

Record Q4FY26: revenue ~₹680 Cr (+26% YoY), PAT ₹101.9 Cr. FY26 ~₹2,180 Cr revenue, ~₹349 Cr profit. Low debt, and a consistent dividend of ₹2.25/share.

Watch

Debtor days have crept from ~71 to ~107 — worth a question to management on collections and order mix before getting comfortable.

Valuation

Priced for perfection

Rich · my own winner

~64× earnings and ~15–16× book. This is not a cheap stock and I won't pretend otherwise. You are paying up for order-book visibility and flawless execution; the margin of safety is thin.

That is precisely why I sold into strength rather than chasing — the price had run ahead of the near-term fundamentals.

Market cap ≈ ₹22,000 Cr · ~64× earnings · ~15–16× book
Technicals

I traded the structure, not the noise

₹885 ATH → ~46% derating to a ₹428 base → a clean results-driven breakout past the ₹675 prior high. I sold the breakout at ₹709.90.

The ₹788 print days later was an intraday blow-off that the stock immediately surrendered — it now sits below my exit. The exit was the structure paying off.

Sector

India's power-capex cycle

A NIFTY Energy constituent levered to the multi-year capex theme — thermal restart, renewables build-out, and broad industrial capex — with a genuine export angle that de-risks the domestic cycle.

The same theme that crushed low-quality mid-caps rewarded the quality leader as earnings landed.

Risk & Behaviour

Why this was conviction, not stubbornness

My broker statement also shows losers I held too long. The distinction matters and I can defend it: Triveni had an improving, fundamentally-anchored thesis and a quality balance sheet — a deliberate hold.

A position with no durable thesis is not conviction; it's a behavioural mistake. Knowing which is which is the whole job.
